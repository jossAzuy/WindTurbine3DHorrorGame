# Wind turbine repairman 3D Horror Game

A video game developed for personal and experimental purposes. The idea for the game originated and was developed while the developer was pursuing a degree in Video Game Programming Engineering at UNIAT, as a personal, non-academic project.

## Description
A short, atmospheric psychological horror game that explores the experience of working as a wind turbine repairman. Throughout the night, you must complete various tasks to keep the turbines running.

You play as a technician starting what appears to be a routine shift. However, as the night progresses, strange events begin to unfold. Something unsettling and supernatural has taken hold of one of the wind turbines… and it won’t rest until it’s finished with you.

## Features
- **Core gameplay:** Repair the aerogenerators and complete simple tasks to progress.
- **Accessible mechanics:** Simple controls combined with an immersive first-person experience.
- **Narrative experience:** A short horror episode with a strong focus on atmosphere and story.
- **Visual Aesthetics:** A terrifying “cinematic” atmosphere with a muted color palette, fog, and subdued lighting.
- **Basic Enemy AI:** A hostile entity that pursues the player, creating constant tension.
