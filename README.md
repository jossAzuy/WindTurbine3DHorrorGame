# Wind turbine repairman 3D Horror Game

A video game developed for personal and experimental purposes. The idea for the game was conceived and developed during my second year of my Video Game Programming Engineering degree at UNIAT as a personal, non-academic project.

## Description
A short atmospheric horror game with a slight narrative focus. You are just another wind turbine repairman until one day you discover that one of them is alive... and will not rest until it has finished you off.

## Features
- Core gameplay loop focused on climbing and repairing wind turbines while ensuring you stay alive.
- 3D single-player experience.
- Narrative-driven environmental horror.
- Aesthetics: Low-saturation color palette, volumetric fog, and restrained lighting focused on mood and environmental storytelling.
- Basic enemy AI focused on player pursuit.
